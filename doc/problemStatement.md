# Problem Statement

## Overview

The **Whole Agent** project aims to provide a seamless end‑to‑end job‑search automation suite. It integrates three complementary micro‑services:

1. **Resume Builder** – Generates polished, ATS‑friendly resumes from user input and extracts key skills.
2. **Job Finder Agent** – Searches multiple job boards, filters listings based on the generated resume, and ranks opportunities.
3. **Email Agent** – Crafts personalized outreach emails and manages follow‑up communication.

These components work together to reduce the manual effort required in a job search, allowing users to focus on interviews and career growth.

## Problem Description

Job seekers spend countless hours:

- Drafting and tailoring resumes for each application.
- Manually searching and filtering job listings across platforms.
- Writing individualized cover letters and follow‑up emails.

The current process is fragmented, error‑prone, and inefficient, leading to missed opportunities and burnout.

## Goals

- **Automation**: Fully automate resume creation, job discovery, and email outreach.
- **Personalization**: Maintain a high degree of personalization to improve response rates.
- **Scalability**: Support multiple users and job platforms without performance degradation.
